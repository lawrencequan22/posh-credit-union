Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and products.

Account Management

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online or visit any of our branches. You will need to provide identification and some basic information to get started. Our friendly staff will guide you through the process and help you choose the right account for your needs.

How do I access my account online?

You can access your account online by logging in through our website. If you haven’t registered for online banking, you can sign up easily on the login page by providing your account details and setting up a secure password.

What should I do if I forget my online banking password?

If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password using your registered email address or phone number.

Can I manage my accounts on mobile?

Yes, you can manage your accounts using our mobile banking app. The app is available for download on both iOS and Android devices. With the app, you can check balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and more.

Payments and Cards

What should I do if I lose my debit or credit card?

If you lose your card, please report it immediately by calling our customer service hotline. We’ll deactivate the lost card to prevent unauthorized transactions and issue a new one to you as soon as possible.

How do I set up direct deposit?

To set up direct deposit, provide your employer with your account number and the Posh Credit Union routing number. You can find these details in your account information online or on your checks.

How do I dispute a transaction on my account?

If you notice a suspicious or unauthorized transaction on your account, contact us immediately. You can dispute the transaction online through your account or by calling our customer service team. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action.

Loans and Mortgages

What documents do I need to apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, you’ll need to provide proof of income, identification, and details about your financial situation. Additional documents may be required depending on the type of loan, such as collateral information for secured loans.

How do I apply for a mortgage?

Applying for a mortgage with Posh Credit Union is easy. You can start your application online, or visit one of our branches for personalized assistance. Our mortgage experts are here to help you through every step of the process, from pre-approval to closing.

How long does it take to get approved for a loan?

The approval time for a loan depends on the type of loan and the completeness of your application. Typically, personal loans and auto loans are approved within a few business days, while mortgage approvals may take longer due to the complexity of the process.

General Information

What are your branch hours?

Our branch hours vary by location, but most branches are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Some branches also offer extended hours on weekends. You can find specific hours for your local branch on our branch locator page.

How do I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team by phone at 1-800-555-1234 or by email at support@poshcu.org. Our representatives are available to assist you during regular business hours.
