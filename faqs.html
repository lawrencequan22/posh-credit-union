Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and products.

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online or visit any of our branches. You will need to provide identification and some basic information to get started.

What documents do I need to apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, you’ll need to provide proof of income, identification, and details about your financial situation. Additional documents may be required depending on the type of loan.

How do I access my account online?

You can access your account online by logging in through our website. If you haven’t registered for online banking, you can sign up easily on the login page.

What should I do if I lose my debit or credit card?

If you lose your card, please report it immediately by calling our customer service hotline. We’ll deactivate the lost card and issue a new one to you as soon as possible.

How do I apply for a mortgage?

Applying for a mortgage with Posh Credit Union is easy. You can start your application online, or visit one of our branches for personalized assistance. Our mortgage experts are here to help you through every step of the process.
