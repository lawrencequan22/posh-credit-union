Membership

Join Posh Credit Union and experience the benefits of being part of our financial family. As a member-owned cooperative, we're committed to your financial success.

Membership Benefits

  • Higher savings rates
  • Lower loan rates
  • Fewer fees
  • Personalized service
  • Financial education
  • Online and mobile banking
  • Nationwide ATM access
  • Community involvement

Membership Eligibility

You're eligible to join Posh Credit Union if you:

  • Live, work, worship, or attend school in [specific geographic area]
  • Are employed by one of our partner companies
  • Have an immediate family member who is already a Posh Credit Union member

Not sure if you're eligible? Contact us and we'll be happy to assist you.

How to Join

  1. Confirm your eligibility
  2. Gather required documents (government-issued ID, proof of address)
  3. Choose your initial deposit amount (minimum $5 to open a savings account)
  4. Complete our online application or visit a branch
  5. Fund your account
