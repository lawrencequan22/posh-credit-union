Our Mission

At Posh Credit Union, our mission is to empower our members with innovative financial solutions, delivered with integrity and a commitment to excellence. We strive to build strong, lasting relationships and to be a trusted partner in your financial journey.

Our Vision

To be the leading credit union, recognized for our exceptional service, innovative products, and unwavering commitment to our members' financial well-being.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency and honesty in all we do.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve our products, services, and processes to better serve our members.
  • Community: We are dedicated to supporting the communities we serve through volunteerism, financial education, and charitable giving.
  • Member Focus: Our members are at the heart of everything we do, and we are committed to helping them achieve their financial goals.

Our History

Founded in 1950, Posh Credit Union has grown from a small community credit union to a trusted financial institution serving thousands of members. Over the decades, we have remained true to our mission of providing personalized service and competitive financial products to help our members succeed.
